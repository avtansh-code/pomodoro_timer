‚ùì Frequently Asked Questions

Does Mr. Pomodoro sync across my devices?

Currently, all your data is stored locally on each device. Multi-device sync features are planned for a future update.

Can I use Siri to control the timer?

Absolutely! Mr. Pomodoro includes Siri Shortcuts support. You can start, pause, resume, or reset your timer using voice commands. Set up shortcuts in the Settings app to customize your commands.

Is my data private and secure?

Yes. All your data stays on your device. We don't collect, track, or sell any personal information. Check our Privacy Policy for full details.

How do I customize timer durations?

Go to Settings in the app to customize your focus time, short break, and long break durations. You can also adjust session counts and enable auto-start features to match your workflow.

Does the app work with Focus Mode?

Yes! Mr. Pomodoro integrates with iOS Focus Modes to help minimize distractions during your focus sessions. Enable this in the app settings to automatically activate your preferred Focus Mode.

How do I report a bug or request a feature?

Use the contact form above or email support@mrpomodoro.com. For bugs, include your iOS version and device model. We respond within 24-48 hours and carefully consider all feature requests.
