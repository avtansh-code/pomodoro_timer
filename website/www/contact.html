‚ùì Frequently Asked Questions

How do I report a bug?

Use the contact form above with "Bug Report" selected, or email support@mrpomodoro.com. Please include your iOS version, device model, and steps to reproduce the issue.

Will you implement my feature request?

We consider all feature requests carefully! While we can't implement every suggestion, popular requests that align with our vision are prioritized for future updates.

How can I get app support?

For general app support, use the contact form or email support@mrpomodoro.com. We're here to help with any questions about using the app.

Do you respond to all messages?

Yes! We read and respond to every message we receive, typically within 24-48 hours. We value your feedback and questions.

Can I suggest UI/design changes?

Absolutely! We love hearing ideas about how to improve the app's interface and user experience. Please be specific about what you'd like to see changed.

Is there a roadmap for future features?

While we don't publish a public roadmap, we're always working on improvements based on user feedback. Follow us for updates on new releases!
